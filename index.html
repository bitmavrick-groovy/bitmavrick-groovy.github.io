Hi-Res Audio

Enjoy high-quality audio playback. Groovy supports almost every popular format including MP3, FLAC, WAV, AAC, OGG, and more, so you never worry about compatibility.

Powerful 5-band EQ

Customize your sound exactly the way you like it with our built-in 5-band equalizer, bass and treble fine-tuning, and personalized audio presets.

Lyrics Support
