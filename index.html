How The App Works?

Permissions

To provide a seamless music experience, the app requires **storage permission** to access audio files on your device. This allows the app to scan, load, and play your favorite songs without any hassle.

Storage

The app efficiently manages your storage by organizing your audio files. You can also **exclude specific folders** to keep unwanted tracks from appearing in your library. Since folder blocking modifies storage access, it requires permission to function properly.

Playlist

Create and manage your own **custom playlists**! Easily add, remove, and reorder songs to personalize your listening experience. Whether it's a workout mix or a chill session, your music is just a tap away.

Lyrics

Enjoy **real-time synced lyrics** while listening to your favorite songs. The app fetches lyrics from an **online API** based on the song's metadata, ensuring accurate and up-to-date lyrics for an immersive music experience.
